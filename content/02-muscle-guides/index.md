# Pillar 02 — Muscle Guides

A per-muscle reference: anatomy and function, how those functions dictate exercise selection, the
regions/heads worth training distinctly, and volume/frequency guidance. Guides are grouped by body
area, and each is backed by structured [`data/muscles/`](../../data/muscles/) files so the future app
can reason about muscle targeting directly.

Each guide covers: anatomy & regions · primary functions (what movements train it) · best exercises by
region and resistance profile · weekly volume landmarks · common mistakes.

## Cross-cutting themes
- **Train a muscle's functions**, not just "a body part" — this is why hamstrings need two exercises and delts need three treatments.
- **Emphasize the lengthened position** (see [Range of Motion](../01-training-variables/range-of-motion.md)).
- **Account for indirect work** — pressing hits front delts and triceps; pulling hits biceps and rear delts.
