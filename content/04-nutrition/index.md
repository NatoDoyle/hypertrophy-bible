# Pillar 04 — Nutrition

The nutritional inputs that support (or limit) muscle growth: energy, protein, and the much smaller
details people over-weight. The guiding theme is a **hierarchy** — energy balance and total protein do
almost all the work; timing and supplements are refinements.

**Status:** ✅ Complete

## The hierarchy (spend your attention in this order)
1. **Calories** — a slight surplus to gain, moderate deficit to cut.
2. **Protein** — ~1.6 g/kg/day, distributed across meals.
3. **Carbs & fat** — fuel training and support health; set by preference within your energy target.
4. **Timing** — minor; hit daily totals with protein around training.
5. **Supplements** — the last 1%; creatine, caffeine, protein powder.
