## The through-line

Most myths share a root error: **over-weighting a minor variable and ignoring the majors.** Timing,
soreness, exercise novelty, specific rep ranges, and hormone spikes get outsized attention, while the
things that actually drive results — **progressive overload, sufficient volume and effort, total
protein and calories, and consistency over time** — get taken for granted. Correct the emphasis and
most myths dissolve.
