## The one-paragraph model

Choose a **split** that lets you train each muscle ~2×/week; fill it with enough **volume** to grow,
started near your minimum and **progressed** upward across a mesocycle via **double progression** or
**RIR autoregulation**; **deload** when fatigue outpaces recovery; keep a **stable core of exercises**
you can overload, rotating accessories occasionally; and **specialize** lagging muscles by trading
volume from elsewhere. Periodization models organize this but don't add much at matched volume — the
engine is progressive overload.
