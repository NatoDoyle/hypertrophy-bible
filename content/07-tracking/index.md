## The one-paragraph model

Steer training by the **leading indicator** — are your **lifts progressing** (load × reps) at a
controlled **RIR**? — and confirm with **lagging outcome** measures (bodyweight trend, tape,
photos) read over months. Keep a **log** so you can beat last week; **autoregulate** effort with
RIR/RPE; and when you truly **plateau**, troubleshoot volume → effort → fatigue → exercise before
concluding you've hit your limit.
