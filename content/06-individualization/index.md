## The one-paragraph model

Start from the general principles, then adjust: scale **volume and complexity up with training age**;
respect that **older adults** grow with real (not token) training while needing recovery/joint care;
train **women** with the same principles as men (expecting similar relative gains); accept that
**genetics** set the rate, ceiling, and shape while training determines how much of that potential you
reach; and ramp **returning lifters** back quickly because muscle memory is real. Medical
circumstances always override general guidance.
