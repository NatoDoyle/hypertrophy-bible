# Pillar 05 — Recovery

Adaptation happens between sessions, not during them. This pillar covers the inputs that determine how
much training you can recover from and turn into growth — and dispels the recovery practices that are
mostly theater.

## The one-paragraph model

You adapt to training during recovery, funded by a **finite, shared budget** that sleep, nutrition,
and life stress fill or drain. Protect **sleep** first; keep total stress in check; **deload** before
fatigue buries your gains. Ignore **soreness** as a progress gauge — it marks damage, not growth — and
don't count on **gadgets** (foam rollers, ice baths) to do what sleep and sensible loads do. The real
recovery tools are unglamorous and effective.
